Home Economics

The Home Economics Club at PSHS - CRC is dedicated to exploring and promoting various aspects of home management and family life. Members engage in activities such as cooking, sewing, and home design. It's a space where students can enhance their practical skills while fostering a sense of community.

Art club

The Art Club is a vibrant and creative community within PSHS - CRC. Here, students with a passion for the arts come together to express themselves through various mediums. From painting to sculpture, the Art Club encourages artistic exploration and provides a platform for students to showcase their talents.

Student Services

Our dedicated Student Services team is committed to providing support and guidance to students. Whether you need assistance with academic concerns, personal challenges, or career planning, we're here to help.

Counseling Services

At PSHS - CRC, we prioritize the mental and emotional well-being of our students. Our counseling services offer a safe and confidential space for students to discuss challenges, seek guidance, and develop coping strategies.

Academic Support

Excel in your studies with the help of our comprehensive academic support programs. From study resources, we provide the tools and assistance you need to succeed in your academic endeavors.

Health and Wellness Programs
